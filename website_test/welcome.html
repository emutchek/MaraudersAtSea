MARAUDERS AT SEA

Once Upon A Time, there was a faraway land called ____. It was a land like any other: friendships to make, glory to seek, grudges to hold. You could find all sorts of people if you sailed long enough, inventors living in deserts and assassins hiding in jungles and singers on drifting islands. But to travel the waves meant risking a run-in with a ship full of pirates. Fueled by rum and strapped with swords, these women lived in infamy for their fearlessness. There was one crew in particular who earned such a reputation that their names continued to be whispered one hundred years after they last treaded the land. The crew had a brave and noble captain, of course, by the name of Eurydice. Her first mate, Cas, was slightly less noble but just as brave - and incredibly dashing. The pair had grown inseparable after meeting on the Isle of Pirates, a trading post for gossiping, carrousing, and selling merchandise of questionable origin. Luckily, they also met a couple level-headed sailors along the way: Amira and Ish, both temporarily applying their intelligence to pirating to earn a few coin. Together, the four of them did decently well for themselves. On one journey, though, they found themselves rescued by a strange woman named Orlic, who happily accepted a post on the crew despite having almost no discernible talents or knowledge of sailing whatsoever. Of couse, this legend began long before those pirates got twisted up in it, leaving whispers in their wake. It’s actually a story you’ve heard before. But maybe, with you here… maybe you’ll tell it a different way.
